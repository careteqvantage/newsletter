ASC Expansion Accelerates — Higher-Acuity Procedures Leading 2026 Growth
ASC IntelligenceHigh Priority

Ambulatory surgery centers continue to absorb higher-complexity orthopedic cases as CMS expands the ASC-covered procedures list for 2026. Volume growth is concentrated in total joint and spine procedures previously requiring hospital stays.

Why It Matters: Michigan MSK practices are positioned to capture this migration from hospital ORs, but payer contracts and staffing plans must be updated to reflect the expanded case mix.
  • Evaluate ASC case mix expansion opportunities for orthopedic service lines
  • Assess capital and staffing requirements for higher-acuity procedure uptake
  • Review payer contracts for updated ASC coverage policies effective 2026

CMS CY 2026 OPPS/ASC Final Rule — April 1 Enforcement Now Active
Regulatory UpdatesHigh Priority

The CY 2026 OPPS and ASC final rule enforcement began April 1, 2026. Key changes include updated payment rates, expanded ASC-covered procedures, and new hospital price transparency requirements.

Why It Matters: Billing systems must reflect April 1 changes immediately. Non-compliance with price transparency requirements carries CMS civil monetary penalties up to $2M/year for large facilities.
  • Confirm billing system updates are live for April 1 rule enforcement
  • Review price transparency posting requirements for all locations
  • Communicate payment rate changes to clinical and administrative staff

Orthopedic Surgeon Supply Projected to Decline 4.3% Through 2037
Workforce & TalentMedium Priority

Federal workforce projections indicate a 4.3% decline in practicing orthopedic surgeons by 2037, driven by retirement-age demographics and insufficient residency pipeline growth.

Why It Matters: Michigan MSK organizations will face escalating compensation pressure competing for a shrinking surgeon pool.
  • Develop proactive physician recruitment strategy targeting Michigan residency programs
  • Evaluate NP/PA utilization models to offset future surgeon capacity constraints
  • Benchmark current compensation against regional and national surveys

Regulatory UpdatesHigh Priority

CMS added cardiac ablation CPT codes to the 2026 ASC covered procedures list, signaling further orthopedic procedure migration likely in CY 2027 rulemaking.

Why It Matters: The regulatory direction confirms CMS intent to keep expanding ASC procedure scope. Anticipate additional complex spine and joint procedures becoming ASC-eligible.
  • Monitor CY 2027 OPPS proposed rule for additional orthopedic ASC additions
  • Assess ASC facility capability for expanding surgical complexity
  • Review anesthesia and post-op monitoring protocols for expanded case types

Biodynamik received FDA IDE approval for its XT3 System, initiating a pivotal U.S. trial targeting diabetic foot ulcer treatment with a novel biomechanical approach.
